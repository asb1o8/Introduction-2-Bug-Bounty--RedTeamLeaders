### The Value of Recon: Thinking Like a Mapper

#### Passive Recon is Stealthy and Rich:-
- Certificate Transparency logs (ex- crt.sh)
- DNS history (ex- SecurityTrails, DNSDumpster)
- Search engines (ex- Google dorks)
- Archive services (ex- Wayback Machine)
- Public repositories (ex- github)

####  Active Recon is High-Fidelity:
- Real-time asset status
- Accurate IP resolution
- Updated technologies
- Open ports and headers

#### Recon is a Continuous Process:- 
- New subdomains get added
- Deployments change structure
- Temporary endpoints appear
- Leaks happen over time

#### Recon Defines Your Success Rate
#### Think Like a Developer, Not a Hacker
#### Prioritize Assets Before Attacking
#### Use Recon to Find Business Logic Weaknesses
#### Document Everything You Discover
