#### Submission Lifecycle: From Discovery to Fix:-
- Target Selection
- Reconnaissance and Discovery
- Exploitation and Impact Evaluation
- Report Submission
- Triage Process
- Fix and Patch
- Disclosure
- Bounty Payment

#### Types of Programs:-
- Public Programs
- Private Programs
- Vulnerability Disclosure Programs

#### Report Lifecycle Status Tags:-
- Submitted: Awaiting triage
- Triaged: Valid and acknowledged
- Duplicate: Already reported
- Informative: Not a bug or out-of-scope
- Resolved: Fixed or mitigated
- Rewarded: Payout approved
- Closed: Report finalized

####  Severity Ratings and Standards:- 
- Low
- Medium
- High
- Critical

##### Severity is judged on:
- Ease of exploitation
- Technical depth
- Scope (e.g., subdomain vs production)
- Data sensitivity (e.g., PII, tokens)
