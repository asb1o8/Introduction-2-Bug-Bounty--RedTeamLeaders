#### Signal
- It is the ratio of valid reports to total reports submitted.

#### Reputation
- Report severity
- Bounty size
- Speed of submission
- Program discretion

#### Traits of a High-Signal Researcher
- Precision
- Clarity
- Research-Oriented
- Consistency
- Patience and Professionalism

#### Tips to Increase Your Signal
- Avoid Duplicate Reports
- Stay in Scope
- Validate Findings Thoroughly
- Explain the Vulnerability Clearly
- Report High-Value, Not High-Volume

#### What Makes a “Good” Report
- Clear summary
- Impact description
- Exploit details
- Fix recommendations
- Professional tone

#### Building Reputation Over Time
#### Signal Killers: 
- Submitting automated scans without triage
- Copy-pasting from public writeups
- Misreporting features as bugs (e.g., CORS misconfigured but intended)
- Repeating low-severity bugs to inflate volume
- Acting hostile or impatient with triagers

#### Signal Beyond Platforms
- Quality blogs on Medium/Blogger. 
- Share tools and techniques you've developed
- Speak at Security Confrences at all possible level.
- Contribute to open-source tools and bounty recon frameworks.

#### 10. Tracking and Improving Yourself
- Log all your reports, accepted/rejected.
- Note recon methodology, tools and RCA.
- Introspect your signal.
- Identify the patterns in valid & invalid findings.
